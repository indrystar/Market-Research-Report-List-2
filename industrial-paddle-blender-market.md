<p><p>Industrial paddle blenders come in different types including paddle-ribbon and double ribbon blenders. Paddle-ribbon blenders have paddle blades that create a fluidized mixing action, ideal for mixing powders, granules, and pastes. Double ribbon blenders, on the other hand, have two helical ribbons that provide more intensive mixing, making them suitable for mixing harder materials or materials that require a more thorough blend. Both types of blenders are commonly used in industries such as food processing, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals.</p></p>
