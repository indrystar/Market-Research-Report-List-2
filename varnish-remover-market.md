<p><p>Varnish removers are categorized into three main types based on their chemical composition: acid, alkaline, and neutral varnish removers. Acid varnish removers work by breaking down and dissolving the varnish through acidic properties. Alkaline varnish removers use alkaline solutions to effectively strip off varnish layers. Neutral varnish removers are formulated with a balanced pH level to gently remove varnish without causing damage to the surface. Each type of varnish remover caters to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
