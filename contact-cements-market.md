<p><p>Contact cements are categorized into different types based on their chemical composition and structure. CR (polychloroprene) contact cements are known for their high strength and durability, making them ideal for bonding a variety of materials. SBS (styrene-butadiene-styrene) contact cements are more flexible and versatile, making them suitable for applications requiring movement. Other types of contact cements include neoprene, acrylic, and polyurethane, each offering unique properties and advantages depending on the specific requirements of the job.</p></p>
