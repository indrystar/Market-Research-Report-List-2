<p><ul><li>Primer & Base Coat</li><li>Top Coat</li><li>Clear Coat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive refinish paint market is segmented into three main types: primer & base coat, top coat, and clear coat. Primer & base coat is used as the first layer to provide adhesion and corrosion protection. Top coat is applied for color and gloss enhancement, while clear coat is used as the final layer for protection and durability. Together, these three types work together to provide a high-quality finish to vehicles, ensuring protection against environmental factors and a long-lasting aesthetic appeal.</p></p>
