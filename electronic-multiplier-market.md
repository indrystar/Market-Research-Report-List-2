<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Electronic Multiplier Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM)</li><li>Continuous-dynode multipliers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Channel Electron Multipliers (CEM) are electronic devices that amplify the number of electrons in a signal by directing them through a series of channels, allowing for high sensitivity and resolution in applications such as mass spectrometry. Continuous-dynode multipliers, on the other hand, use a continuous chain of dynodes to amplify the signal by accelerating electrons through multiple stages of multiplication. Both types of multipliers are essential in various scientific and industrial applications requiring high levels of sensitivity and precision.</p></p>
