<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Strain Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Strain Sensor</li><li>Transverse Strain Sensor</li><li>Longitudinal Strain Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strain sensors can be categorized into three main types: surface strain sensors, transverse strain sensors, and longitudinal strain sensors. </p><p>Surface strain sensors measure strain on the surface of an object, providing a high level of accuracy in detecting deformation. </p><p>Transverse strain sensors measure strain perpendicular to the surface of an object, ideal for applications requiring measurement of strain in multiple directions. </p><p>Longitudinal strain sensors measure strain along the length of an object, suitable for applications where stress and strain need to be monitored in a specific direction.</p></p>
