<p><p>Double Telecentric lenses are used in machine vision systems for measuring and inspecting objects with high precision. These lenses come in two main market types: C-Type Interface Lens and F-Type Interface Lens. C-Type Interface Lenses are designed for cameras with a C-mount interface, commonly used in industrial applications. F-Type Interface Lenses, on the other hand, are designed for cameras with an F-mount interface, which is typically found in high-end digital SLR cameras. Both types of lenses provide telecentricity, ensuring accurate and consistent imaging results.</p></p>
