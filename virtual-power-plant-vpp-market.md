<p><p>Virtual Power Plant (VPP) market types include the OC Model and FM Model. The OC Model is a model where VPP operators aggregate distributed energy resources to sell power on the open market. The FM Model involves participating in frequency regulation markets to provide grid stability services. Both market types allow for more efficient utilization of renewable energy resources by aggregating smaller assets into a larger, more flexible virtual power plant.</p></p>
