<p><ul><li>Solvent-Based Film</li><li>Water-Based Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Color print film market consists of two main types - solvent-based film and water-based film. Solvent-based film is made using chemicals dissolved in solvents, providing a durable and long-lasting finish. On the other hand, water-based film is made using water as the main carrier, making it a more environmentally friendly option. Solvent-based film is known for its high resistance to scratches and chemicals, while water-based film is preferred for its low odor and easier cleanup. Both types cater to different customer preferences and project requirements.</p></p>
