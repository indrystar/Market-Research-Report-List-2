<p><p>The Automatic Microtome market is segmented into different types based on the techniques used for tissue or specimen sectioning. Traditional Histology Technique involves paraffin embedding and thin sectioning for routine histological analysis. Cryosectioning Technique uses freezing temperatures to cut specimens for quick analysis without the need for fixation or processing. Electron Microscopy Technique produces ultrathin sections of specimens for viewing under an electron microscope. Botanical Microtomy Technique specializes in sectioning plant tissues for botanical research and analysis.</p></p>
