<p><p>Dental zirconia blocks come in three different types based on their translucency levels. Low Translucency Zirconia Blocks are highly opaque and suitable for crowns and bridges in the back of the mouth. Medium Translucency Zirconia Blocks provide a balance between strength and aesthetics, ideal for crowns and bridges in visible areas. High Translucency Zirconia Blocks offer superior aesthetics and natural translucency, making them perfect for front tooth restorations. Each type of zirconia block caters to specific dental needs and preferences.</p></p>
