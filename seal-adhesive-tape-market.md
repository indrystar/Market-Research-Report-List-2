<p><p>Seal adhesive tape market consists of two main types: single side and double side. Single side adhesive tapes have adhesive applied on just one side, commonly used for sealing packaging and boxes. Double side adhesive tapes have adhesive on both sides, ideal for bonding two surfaces together. Single side tapes are more commonly used for surface protection and mounting, while double side tapes are more versatile and commonly used in industries like automotive, construction, and electronics for secure bonding.</p></p>
