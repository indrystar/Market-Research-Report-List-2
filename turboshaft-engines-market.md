<p><p>Turboshaft engines can be categorized into two main types: Axial-Flow Type and Centrifugal Type. Axial-Flow Type turbines have a series of rotating blades that operate in a straight line, allowing for higher efficiency and power output. Centrifugal Type turbines, on the other hand, use a rotating disk with blades that operate in a radial direction, providing simpler construction and lower costs. Both types cater to different market needs, with Axial-Flow Type engines typically being used in larger helicopters and aircraft, while Centrifugal Type engines are commonly found in smaller aircraft and drones.</p></p>
