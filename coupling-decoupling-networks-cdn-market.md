<p><p>Coupling Decoupling Networks (CDN) include two main market types: Automatic Coupling Decoupling Networks and Manual Coupling Decoupling Networks. Automatic CDNs are designed to quickly and efficiently connect and disconnect devices without human intervention, making them ideal for streamlined operations. On the other hand, Manual CDNs require human interaction for connecting and disconnecting devices, offering more control and flexibility in complex setups.
