<p><p>Bee smokers are crucial tools in beekeeping for calming bees during hive inspections. There are two main types of bee smokers: manual bee smokers and electric bee smokers. Manual bee smokers require the beekeeper to generate smoke by using fuel such as wood chips or cotton. On the other hand, electric bee smokers run on batteries and produce smoke with the push of a button. Both types serve the same purpose of calming bees, but offer different levels of convenience for beekeepers.</p></p>
