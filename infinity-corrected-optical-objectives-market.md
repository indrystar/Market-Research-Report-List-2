<p><p>Infinity Corrected Optical Objectives are commonly available in three main market types: 165mm, 180mm, and 200mm. These market types refer to the working distance between the objective lens and the specimen being observed. The 165mm market type offers a shorter working distance, ideal for applications requiring high magnification. The 200mm market type offers a longer working distance, making it suitable for applications where space is limited or where manipulation of the specimen is necessary. The 180mm market type falls in between, offering a balanced working distance for various applications.</p></p>
