<p><p>Magnesium Sulphate market types include Heptahydrate (Epsom Salt), Anhydrous (Calcined Kieserite), and Monohydrate (Kieserite). Heptahydrate, commonly known as Epsom Salt, is a hydrated form of magnesium sulphate often used in bath salts and medical treatments. Anhydrous magnesium sulphate, also known as Calcined Kieserite, is a dry form of the compound used in fertilizers and industrial applications. Monohydrate, or Kieserite, is a form of magnesium sulphate with one molecule of water, also utilized in agriculture for soil improvement.</p></p>
