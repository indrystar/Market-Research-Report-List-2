<p><ul><li>Continuous</li><li>Quantitative</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Powder dosing feeders are available in two main types: continuous and quantitative. Continuous powder dosing feeders are designed to continuously dispense a constant flow of powder materials without interruption. On the other hand, quantitative powder dosing feeders are designed to dispense a specific, predetermined amount of powder material at a time. Each type caters to different industrial requirements, with continuous feeders being ideal for applications requiring a steady flow of powder and quantitative feeders being suitable for precise dosing needs.</p></p>
