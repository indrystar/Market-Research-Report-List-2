<p><p>High purity tungsten sheets are commonly available in two main types in the market, namely alloy and pure metal. Alloy tungsten sheets are made by combining tungsten with other metals to enhance certain properties such as strength or heat resistance, making them suitable for specific industrial applications. On the other hand, pure metal tungsten sheets are made entirely of tungsten without any other elements, offering high purity levels and exceptional thermal and electrical conductivity, ideal for applications where purity is critical such as in electronics or semiconductor industries.</p></p>
