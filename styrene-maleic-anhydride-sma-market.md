<p><p>Styrene Maleic Anhydride (SMA) can be classified into two main types in the market: Alternate Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer and Random Styrene-Maleic Anhydride Copolymer. Alternate SMA copolymers have a regular alternated structure of styrene and maleic anhydride units, while random SMA copolymers have a random distribution of these units along the polymer chain. Both types of SMA copolymers have unique properties and find applications in various industries such as adhesives, coatings, and biomedical materials.</p></p>
