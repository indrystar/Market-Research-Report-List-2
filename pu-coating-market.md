<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The PU Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-borne</li><li>Water-borne</li><li>High Solids</li><li>Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PU coatings are available in various types, including solvent-borne, water-borne, high solids, and powder formulations. Solvent-borne coatings contain volatile organic compounds and require proper ventilation during application. Water-borne coatings are environmentally friendly and easy to clean up. High solids coatings have a high solid content and provide excellent durability. Powder coatings are applied as a dry powder and then baked to form a hard finish. Each type of PU coating offers unique advantages and is suitable for different applications.</p></p>
