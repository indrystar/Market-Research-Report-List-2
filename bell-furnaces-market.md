<p><p>Bell furnaces come in two main market types: electrically heated and gas fired. Electrically heated bell furnaces use electricity to generate heat for various industrial processes, offering ease of use and control. On the other hand, gas fired bell furnaces utilize natural gas or propane as a fuel source to heat up the furnace chamber, providing efficient heating capabilities. Both types cater to different needs in the market, with electrically heated furnaces being more common in smaller scale operations and gas fired furnaces usually used in larger industrial settings.</p></p>
