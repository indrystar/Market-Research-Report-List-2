<p><ul><li>Intermittent Motion Bundling Machine</li><li>Continuous Motion Bundling Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bundling machines can be categorized into two main types: Intermittent Motion Bundling Machine and Continuous Motion Bundling Machine. Intermittent motion machines bundle products by pausing between each product grouping, making them ideal for smaller production runs. On the other hand, continuous motion machines bundle products without pausing, allowing for higher production rates and efficiency in larger scale operations. Each type has its own advantages depending on the production requirements and output volumes of the business.</p></p>
