<p><p>UV Enhanced Photodiodes Market offers two main types: Inversion Channel Series and Planar Diffused Series. </p><p>Inversion Channel Series photodiodes utilize a metal-on-silicon process to create a high-quality ultraviolet-sensitive diode with improved quantum efficiency and lower dark current. </p><p>Planar Diffused Series photodiodes, on the other hand, use a standard diffused junction process to achieve similar UV sensitivity but with a simpler manufacturing method. </p><p>Both types cater to different market needs and applications, providing options for customers based on their requirements and budget constraints.</p></p>
