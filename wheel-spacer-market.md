<p><p>Wheel spacers are commonly made from various materials such as paper, rubber, silicone rubber, metal, cork, plastic polymer, and fiberglass. Paper wheel spacers are lightweight and cost-effective, while rubber and silicone rubber spacers provide durability and flexibility. Metal spacers are known for their strength and longevity, while cork spacers offer good vibration damping properties. Plastic polymer spacers are popular due to their lightweight and corrosion resistance. Fiberglass spacers are used for their high strength-to-weight ratio and resistance to temperature and chemical exposure.</p></p>
