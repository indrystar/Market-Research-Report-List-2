<p><p>Scroll saws in the market are typically categorized into two main types: C-arm and parallel arm designs. C-arm scroll saws have a single pivot point for the arm, allowing for a more compact and space-saving design. On the other hand, parallel arm scroll saws have two parallel arms that move up and down simultaneously, providing more stability and control when cutting intricate designs. Each type has its own unique advantages and is preferred by different users depending on their specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
