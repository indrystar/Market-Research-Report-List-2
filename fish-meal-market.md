<p><p>Fish meal market can be categorized into three types based on the processing method: Whole fish meal, Semi-skimmed fish meal, and Defatted fish meal. Whole fish meal is made from whole fish and contains high levels of protein and oil. Semi-skimmed fish meal is processed to remove some of the oil content, while Defatted fish meal undergoes further processing to reduce both oil and fat levels. These types of fish meal cater to different market demands and applications in various industries such as aquaculture, animal feed, and agriculture.</p></p>
