<p><p>Non-Filtered Pipette Tips refer to the standard type of pipette tips used for general liquid handling applications. They do not have any additional filtration capabilities. Filtered Pipette Tips, on the other hand, come with built-in filters to prevent aerosol and liquid contamination, making them ideal for applications where sample purity is crucial. Both types cater to different market needs and preferences, with non-filtered tips being more commonly used in routine laboratory work and filtered tips preferred for sensitive experiments and applications.</p></p>
