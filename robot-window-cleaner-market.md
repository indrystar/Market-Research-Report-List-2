<p><ul><li>Vacuum Suction</li><li>Fan Adsorption</li><li>Brushless Motor Adsorption</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Robot window cleaner market types include vacuum suction, fan adsorption, and brushless motor adsorption. Vacuum suction robots utilize a strong vacuum to attach to and clean windows. Fan adsorption robots use fans to create suction and adhere to glass surfaces. Brushless motor adsorption robots employ brushless motors to create the suction needed to stick to windows. Each type provides different strengths and capabilities, catering to various customer needs and preferences in the window cleaning industry.</p></p>
