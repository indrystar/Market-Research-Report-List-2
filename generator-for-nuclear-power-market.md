<p><p>Generator for Nuclear Power market consists of three main types: Pressurized Water Reactor (PWR), Water-Water Energetic Reactor（WWER), and Pressurized Heavy Water Reactor (PHWR). PWRs are the most common type globally, utilizing water as a coolant and moderator. WWERs are commonly used in Eastern Europe, also using water as a coolant and moderator. PHWRs use heavy water as a moderator and coolant, allowing for the use of natural uranium as fuel. Each type has its own unique characteristics and market presence.</p></p>
