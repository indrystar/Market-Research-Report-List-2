<p><p>Bioadhesives are a type of adhesive that are derived from natural sources and are considered environmentally friendly alternatives to traditional petroleum-based adhesives. In the packaging industry, there are two main types of bioadhesives: animal-based and plant-based. Animal-based bioadhesives are derived from animal proteins like collagen, gelatin, or albumin. Plant-based bioadhesives, on the other hand, are derived from plant sources such as starch, cellulose, or lignin. Both types offer strong adhesive properties and are biodegradable, making them ideal for sustainable packaging solutions.</p></p>
