<p><p>Power Quality Meters are devices used to monitor and analyze the electrical system for any disturbances or quality issues. They come in two main types: Single Phase and Three Phase meters. Single Phase meters are designed for smaller residential or small commercial applications, while Three Phase meters are used in larger industrial or commercial settings. Both types of meters measure parameters such as voltage, current, power factor, harmonics, and frequency to ensure the electrical system is operating efficiently and reliably.</p></p>
