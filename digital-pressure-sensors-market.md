<p><strong>The Digital Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Digital Pressure Sensors</li><li>Absolut Digital Pressure Sensors</li><li>Differential Digital Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Pressure Sensors market can be classified into three main types: Relative Digital Pressure Sensors, Absolute Digital Pressure Sensors, and Differential Digital Pressure Sensors. Relative Digital Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, while Absolute Digital Pressure Sensors measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum. Differential Digital Pressure Sensors measure the difference in pressure between two points. Each type of sensor is used in various applications where accurate pressure measurement is required, such as in industrial processes, automotive systems, and medical devices.</p></p>
