<p><p>Radial tires and bias tires are two main types in the tires market. Radial tires have steel belts running across the tread in a radial direction, providing better traction, fuel efficiency, and longer tread life. Bias tires, on the other hand, have crisscrossed ply layers, offering a more affordable option with a smoother ride quality. Both types have their own advantages and are used in different vehicles based on specific needs and preferences.</p></p>
