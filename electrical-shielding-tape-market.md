<p><p>Electrical shielding tapes are categorized into two main types: adhesive shielding tapes and non-adhesive shielding tapes. Adhesive shielding tapes come with a sticky backing that allows for easy application on various surfaces, providing a secure shield against electromagnetic interference. On the other hand, non-adhesive shielding tapes do not have a sticky backing and are generally used for wrapping around cables or wires to provide electromagnetic shielding. Both types of shielding tapes are essential in industries where protection against electromagnetic interference is crucial.</p></p>
