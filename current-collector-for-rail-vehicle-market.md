<p><strong>The Current Collector for Rail Vehicle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Top-Running</li><li>Bottom-Running</li><li>Side-Running</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Top-running, bottom-running, and side-running are different types of current collectors used in rail vehicles. </p><p>Top-running collectors are mounted on the roof of the vehicle and collect electricity from overhead wires. </p><p>Bottom-running collectors are located underneath the vehicle and collect power from a third rail. </p><p>Side-running collectors are positioned on the side of the vehicle and collect power from a conductor rail. </p><p>Each type of collector has its own advantages and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the rail system.</p></p>
