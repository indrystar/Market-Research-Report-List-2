<p><p>Commercial waffle makers are available in two main types: single-grid and double-grid. Single-grid waffle makers produce one waffle at a time, making them ideal for smaller establishments or low-volume usage. On the other hand, double-grid waffle makers can produce two waffles simultaneously, increasing efficiency and output for busier establishments. Both types offer varying features and capabilities, such as adjustable temperature settings and non-stick surfaces, to cater to the specific needs of commercial kitchens and foodservice businesses.</p></p>
