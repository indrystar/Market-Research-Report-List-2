<p><p>High temperature fibers can be categorized into different types such as Aramid fibers (including Para-aramid and Meta-aramid) known for their high strength and heat resistance, and Ceramic fibers (including Refractory Ceramic Fibers and Low Bio-persistent fibers) used for their thermal insulation properties. Other types of high temperature fibers include carbon fibers, silicon carbide fibers, and polybenzimidazole fibers which also offer excellent heat resistance properties. These fibers are commonly used in industries such as aerospace, automotive, and defense for their ability to withstand extreme temperatures.</p></p>
