IBCs are large containers used for transporting and storing liquids and granulated substances. FIBCs, also known as bulk bags or jumbo bags, are flexible packaging containers used for storing and shipping dry, flowable products. Both types are reusable and designed for multiple trips, making them cost-effective and environmentally friendly options for businesses in need of durable and efficient packaging solutions.</p></p>
