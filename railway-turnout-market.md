<p><p>Railway turnout market consists of various types including Single Crossover, Double Crossover, Track Crossing, Slip Turnout, and Lapped Turnouts. Single Crossover turnouts allow trains to switch tracks in a single direction. Double Crossover turnouts enable trains to switch tracks in two directions. Track Crossing turnouts allow tracks to intersect at various angles. Slip Turnouts provide a way for trains to enter or exit a siding track. Lapped Turnouts are used in congested areas to allow for smoother transitions between tracks.</p></p>
