<p><p>Military Specification Resistor Market Types include Through Hole and Surface Mount. Through Hole resistors have leads that are inserted through holes on a circuit board and soldered to secure them in place. These types are known for their durability and reliability in harsh environments. Surface Mount resistors are soldered directly onto the surface of the circuit board, allowing for more compact and lightweight designs. Both types are commonly used in military and aerospace applications that require high performance and reliability.</p></p>
