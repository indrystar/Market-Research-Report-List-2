<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Mobile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>LNG vaporizers can be classified into two main types: static and mobile. Static vaporizers are stationary units installed at LNG terminals or industrial facilities to convert liquid LNG into gaseous form. These are commonly used in large-scale operations with high capacity requirements. On the other hand, mobile vaporizers are portable units that can be transported to remote locations or temporary sites for LNG re-gasification. They offer flexibility and convenience for smaller-scale operations or for emergency situations where a quick and temporary solution is needed.</p></p>
