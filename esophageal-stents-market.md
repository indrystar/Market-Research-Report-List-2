<p><p>Esophageal stents are used to treat obstructions in the esophagus. The market for esophageal stents includes two main types: Self-expandable Metallic Stents (SEMS) and Self-expanding Plastic Stents (SEPS). SEMS are made of metal and provide structural support to the esophagus, while SEPS are made of plastic and are flexible. SEMS are preferred for long-term use as they are more durable, while SEPS are easier to remove and replace. Both types of stents are effective in relieving symptoms and improving quality of life for patients with esophageal obstructions.</p></p>
