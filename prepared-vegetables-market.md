<p><p>The prepared vegetables market is divided into three main types: Ready to Eat (RTE), Ready to Heat (RTH), and Ready to Cook (RTC). RTE vegetables are fully cooked and ready to consume, requiring no additional preparation. RTH vegetables are partially cooked and just need to be heated before eating. RTC vegetables are raw or minimally processed, requiring cooking from scratch. These different market types cater to consumers looking for convenience, quick meal solutions, or those who prefer to cook from fresh ingredients.</p></p>
