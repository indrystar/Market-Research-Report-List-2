<p><ul><li>ISO 14001</li><li>EMAS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Environmental Management System or EMS includes ISO 14001 and EMAS as different market types. ISO 14001 is a standard established by the International Organization for Standardization to help organizations improve their environmental performance. It focuses on reducing environmental impacts and complying with regulations. On the other hand, EMAS (Eco-Management and Audit Scheme) is a voluntary scheme in the European Union that goes beyond ISO 14001 by requiring organizations to undergo third-party verification and publicly report their environmental performance. Both systems aim to promote sustainable practices and reduce environmental harm.</p></p>
