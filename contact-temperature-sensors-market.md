<p><p>Contact temperature sensors are available in two main market types: Wired Type and Wireless Type. Both types are commonly used in industrial, commercial, and residential settings to ensure accurate temperature measurement and control in various environments. The choice between wired and wireless types depends on the specific application requirements and preferences.</p></p>
