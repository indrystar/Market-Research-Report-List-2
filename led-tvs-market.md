<p><p>LED TVs are available in two market types based on display patterns: 2D and 3D. In 2D LED TVs, the display is two-dimensional, offering a traditional viewing experience without added depth perception. On the other hand, 3D LED TVs provide a more immersive viewing experience by creating the illusion of depth. This is achieved through specialized glasses or screen technology that allows viewers to perceive images as if they were three-dimensional. Both market types cater to different preferences and viewing experiences.</p></p>
