<p><p>POU (Point of Use) All-In-One RO Water Purifiers are designed to be installed at specific outlets where water is required, such as under the sink or on the countertop. They are ideal for individual households or small offices. On the other hand, POE (Point of Entry) All-In-One RO Water Purifiers are installed at the main water source of a building, providing purified water to the entire property. They are suitable for larger establishments like commercial buildings or residential complexes.</p></p>
