<p><p>Synthetic & Bio-Based Coatings can be categorized into two main types: Internal and External coatings. Internal coatings are applied on the interior surfaces of a product or structure, providing protection from corrosion, wear and tear, and improving aesthetics. External coatings, on the other hand, are applied on the exterior surfaces to shield against environmental factors such as UV radiation, moisture, and chemicals. Both types of coatings are widely used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and aerospace for enhancing durability and performance of products.</p></p>
