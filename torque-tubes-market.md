<p><p>Torque tubes are commonly used in various industries for transmitting rotary motion. There are two main types of torque tubes: Inner Torque Tube and Outer Torque Tube. Inner torque tubes are located within a shaft or casing, while outer torque tubes are positioned outside of a shaft or casing. Inner torque tubes are typically used for applications requiring precision and compactness, while outer torque tubes are better suited for applications where space is not a constraint and higher torques need to be transmitted.</p></p>
