<p><strong>The Button Cell Battery Holder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Mount</li><li>Through Hole</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Button Cell Battery Holder market consists of two main types: Surface Mount and Through Hole. Surface Mount holders are soldered directly onto the surface of the circuit board, allowing for a more compact design and easier automated assembly. Through Hole holders require the leads to be inserted through holes in the board and soldered on the other side, offering a stronger mechanical connection but requiring more space. Manufacturers may choose between these two types based on design requirements, production capabilities, and cost considerations.</p></p>
