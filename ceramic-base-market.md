<p><p>Ceramic bases can be classified into different types based on their material composition. These include Alumina (Al2O3), Aluminium Nitride (AlN), Beryllium Oxide (BeO), Silicon Nitride (Si3N4), and others. Alumina is known for its high thermal conductivity and resistance to chemicals, while Aluminium Nitride offers excellent thermal conductivity. Beryllium Oxide has high thermal conductivity and low dielectric loss, and Silicon Nitride is valued for its high strength and thermal shock resistance. Other ceramic base materials offer unique properties for various applications.</p></p>
