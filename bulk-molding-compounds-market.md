<p><ul><li>Glass Fiber</li><li>Carbon Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bulk molding compounds (BMC) are available in two main types: glass fiber and carbon fiber. Glass fiber BMC is reinforced with glass fibers, providing strength and stiffness to the final product. On the other hand, carbon fiber BMC is reinforced with carbon fibers, offering even greater strength and lighter weight compared to glass fiber BMC. Both types of BMC are widely used in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction for their excellent mechanical properties and versatility in manufacturing complex shapes.</p></p>
