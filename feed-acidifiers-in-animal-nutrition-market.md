<p><p>Feed acidifiers are additives used in animal nutrition to promote optimum gut health and improve feed efficiency. There are three main types of feed acidifiers: compound acidifiers, organic acidifiers, and inorganic acidifiers. Compound acidifiers are blends of various organic acids and salts, organic acidifiers are naturally occurring acids like formic acid and propionic acid, and inorganic acidifiers are minerals like copper and zinc. Each type of acidifier offers unique benefits and applications in animal nutrition.</p></p>
