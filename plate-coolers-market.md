<p><p>Plate coolers are commonly used in industries for transferring heat between two fluids. Two popular types of plate coolers are frame coolers and brazed coolers. Frame coolers consist of a frame with separate plates that can be easily removed for cleaning or replacement. On the other hand, brazed coolers have plates that are permanently sealed together using brazing. Frame coolers are ideal for applications where customization and flexibility are required, while brazed coolers are preferred for their durability and high thermal efficiency.</p></p>
