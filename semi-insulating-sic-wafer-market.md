<p><p>Semi insulating SiC wafers are commonly available in two main types: 4H and 6H. The 4H market focuses on wafers with a hexagonal crystal structure, offering high electron mobility and thermal conductivity. On the other hand, the 6H market features wafers with a rhombohedral crystal structure, providing better thermal stability and lower defects. Both types cater to different applications in industries such as power electronics, telecommunications, and aerospace, where the unique properties of each crystal structure play a crucial role in performance.</p></p>
