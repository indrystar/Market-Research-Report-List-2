<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) is a versatile thermoplastic polymer used in various industries. There are three main types of Polypropylene based on the molecular structure: Isotactic Polypropylene, Atactic Polypropylene, and Syndiotactic Polypropylene. Isotactic Polypropylene has a high degree of crystallinity and is the most common type used in applications requiring high stiffness and strength. Atactic Polypropylene is an amorphous polymer with low crystallinity, making it flexible and easy to process. Syndiotactic Polypropylene has a unique polymer chain arrangement, providing a balance of stiffness and impact resistance.</p></p>
