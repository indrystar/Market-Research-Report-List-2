<p><p>The graphics card market can be divided into three main types: HD graphics, integrated graphics, and discrete graphics. HD graphics refer to integrated graphics processors that are built into the CPU, providing basic visual performance for everyday tasks. Integrated graphics are typically found in budget laptops and desktops. Discrete graphics, on the other hand, are standalone cards that offer superior performance and are used in gaming PCs and workstations. Discrete graphics cards provide more power and dedicated memory for demanding applications and high-quality gaming experiences.</p></p>
