<p><p>Home audio amplifiers are available in several different classes, each with its own unique characteristics. Class-A amplifiers offer high sound quality but are inefficient and produce a lot of heat. Class-B amplifiers are more efficient but can suffer from distortion issues. Class-A/B amplifiers combine the best of both worlds, offering a balance of quality and efficiency. Class-G and H amplifiers use multiple power supplies to improve efficiency. Class-D amplifiers are the most efficient, using digital technology to produce high-quality sound while minimizing heat output.</p></p>
