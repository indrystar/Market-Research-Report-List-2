<p><p>Nursing call systems are designed to help patients in healthcare facilities contact nurses for assistance quickly and efficiently. Wired nursing call systems require physical cables to connect the nurse call stations to a central monitoring station. On the other hand, wireless nursing call systems use radio frequency technology to transmit calls wirelessly, providing more flexibility in installation and mobility. Both types of systems aim to improve response times and overall patient satisfaction in healthcare settings.</p></p>
