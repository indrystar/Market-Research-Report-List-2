<p><strong>The Car Muffler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bullet Mufflers</li><li>Chambered Mufflers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bullet mufflers are straight-through mufflers that provide minimal restriction to exhaust flow, resulting in a louder and more aggressive exhaust note. They are popular among performance enthusiasts looking for maximum horsepower gains and a race car sound. On the other hand, chambered mufflers feature a series of chambers and baffles that reduce noise levels and provide a deep, smooth exhaust tone. They are preferred by those looking for a quieter and more refined exhaust sound while still improving performance.</p></p>
