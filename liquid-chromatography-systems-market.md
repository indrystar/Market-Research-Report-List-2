<p><p>Liquid Chromatography Systems Market Types include Liquid-Liquid Chromatography (LLC) and Liquid-Solid Chromatography (LSC). LLC separates compounds based on their solubility in two immiscible liquids, while LSC separates compounds based on their affinity for a solid stationary phase. LLC is commonly used for separating non-polar compounds, while LSC is used for separating polar and charged compounds. Both types of chromatography systems play a crucial role in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, environmental analysis, and research laboratories.</p></p>
