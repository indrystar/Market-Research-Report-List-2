<p><p>Explosion Isolation Valves are installed in systems handling combustible dust or gases to prevent explosions from spreading. Horizontal Explosion Isolation Valves are designed for horizontal pipelines where the valve closes to stop the propagation of flames and pressure. Vertical Explosion Isolation Valves are suitable for vertical pipelines, shutting off to prevent deflagration from traveling upwards. Both types of valves play a crucial role in ensuring the safety of industrial processes by containing explosions and minimizing potential damage to equipment and personnel.</p></p>
