<p><p>Medium Chain Triglycerides (MCT) are dietary fats with shorter chain length compared to long-chain triglycerides. MCTs are available in two main types - oil MCT and powder MCT. Oil MCTs are liquid fats derived from coconut or palm kernel oil, while powder MCTs are usually spray-dried and more convenient for use in various applications such as beverages, baked goods, and sports supplements. Both oil and powder MCTs are increasingly popular for their health benefits and potential weight management properties.</p></p>
