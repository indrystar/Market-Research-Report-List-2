<p><p>FIBC Bulk Liners are available in various types to cater to different needs. EVOH Liners are used for products that require oxygen barrier properties. Foil Liners provide protection against moisture and light. Baffled Liners have internal baffles that maintain the shape of the liner during filling. Antistatic Liners are designed to prevent the buildup of static electricity. Conductive Liners are used for products sensitive to static discharge. There are also other liners in the market that offer specific features to suit unique requirements.</p></p>
