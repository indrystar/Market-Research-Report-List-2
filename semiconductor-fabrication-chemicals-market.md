<p><p>Semiconductor fabrication chemicals are categorized into three types based on their physical state: gas, solid, and liquid. Gas chemicals are commonly used in processes such as chemical vapor deposition. Solid chemicals, typically in the form of pellets or powders, are used in processes like ion implantation. Liquid chemicals, in the form of solutions or slurries, are used in processes such as etching and cleaning. Each type of chemical plays a crucial role in the semiconductor fabrication process, contributing to the overall efficiency and effectiveness of chip manufacturing.</p></p>
