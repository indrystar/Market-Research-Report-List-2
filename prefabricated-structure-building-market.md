<p><strong>The Prefabricated Structure Building Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Skeleton System</li><li>Panel System</li><li>Cellular System</li><li>Combined System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Prefabricated structure building market is categorized into four types: skeleton system, panel system, cellular system, and combined system. Skeleton system uses a framework of vertical and horizontal members to support the structure. Panel system uses precast panels for walls, floors, and roofs. Cellular system consists of modules or cells that are assembled on site. Combined system combines elements from the other three types to create a versatile and customizable building solution. Each type offers unique benefits in terms of construction speed, cost-effectiveness, and design flexibility.</p></p>
