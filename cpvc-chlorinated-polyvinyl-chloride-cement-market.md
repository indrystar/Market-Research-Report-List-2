<p><p>CPVC cement is a type of adhesive used for joining CPVC pipes and fittings. There are four main types of CPVC cement: regular bodied, medium bodied, heavy bodied, and extra heavy bodied. Regular bodied CPVC cements are suitable for most general applications, while medium bodied cements are ideal for larger diameter pipes. Heavy bodied cements provide extra strength and durability, and extra heavy bodied cements offer even greater bonding capabilities for demanding or high-pressure applications. Each type of cement is designed to accommodate different project requirements.</p></p>
