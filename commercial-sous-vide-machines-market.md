<p><p>Commercial sous vide machines are divided into two main types: immersion types and water bath types. Immersion types are portable devices that can be used with any container, making them versatile for various cooking tasks. Water bath types consist of a self-contained unit with a built-in water bath, providing precise temperature control and uniform heating. Both types cater to different needs of the foodservice industry, offering efficiency, consistency, and convenience for high-volume cooking operations.</p></p>
