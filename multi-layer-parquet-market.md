<p><ul><li>Glued</li><li>Floating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multi-layer parquet flooring is available in two types of markets: glued and floating. In the glued market, the individual layers of the parquet are bonded together using adhesive, creating a stable and durable floor. On the other hand, in the floating market, the individual layers are not glued to the subfloor but instead interlock with each other, allowing for easy installation and removal. Both markets offer high-quality multi-layer parquet options that cater to different preferences and needs.</p></p>
