<p><p>A vacuum filter is a filtration equipment used to separate solid particles from a liquid or a gas by creating a vacuum. Vacuum filters are commonly used in industries such as chemical processing, pharmaceuticals, water treatment, food and beverage, and mining.</p><p>The Vacuum Filter Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.3% during the forecast period.
