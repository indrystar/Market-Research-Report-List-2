<p><p>External Thermal Insulation System (ETIS) is a type of insulation system used to improve the energy efficiency of buildings. It involves applying an insulating layer to the external walls of a building, which helps to reduce heat loss and increase thermal comfort. ETIS also helps to regulate indoor temperatures, leading to lower energy consumption and reduced heating and cooling costs.</p><p>The External Thermal Insulation System Market is experiencing rapid growth, with a projected CAGR of 13.2% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to the increasing focus on energy efficiency and sustainability in the construction industry. Governments around the world are implementing regulations and incentives to promote the use of energy-efficient building materials, including ETIS. Additionally, rising awareness about the environmental impact of buildings is driving the demand for energy-efficient solutions like ETIS.</p><p>The latest trends in the External Thermal Insulation System Market include the development of advanced materials with improved thermal performance, such as aerogel-based insulation and vacuum insulation panels. There is also a growing emphasis on the integration of ETIS with other building systems, such as ventilation and heating systems, to create more energy-efficient and sustainable buildings. Overall, the demand for ETIS is expected to continue growing as the construction industry prioritizes energy efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
