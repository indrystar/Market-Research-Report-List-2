<p><p>Automotive Engine Cooling Fans come in three main types: Mechanical, Electric, and Other. Mechanical fans are driven by the engine through a belt or pulley system and rely on the engine's rotation to operate. Electric fans, on the other hand, are powered by an electric motor and can be controlled independently of the engine. Other types of cooling fans may include hybrid systems or advanced technologies like variable speed fans. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the automotive industry.</p></p>
