<p><p>PVC Cling Films are classified into four main types based on their thickness: Up to 10 microns, 10-15 microns, 15-20 microns, and above 20 microns. Films up to 10 microns are commonly used for light packaging needs, while those in the 10-15 microns range are ideal for medium-duty applications. Films in the 15-20 microns category offer more durability and strength, suitable for heavier packaging requirements. Films above 20 microns are the thickest and provide the highest level of protection, making them ideal for industrial use.</p></p>
