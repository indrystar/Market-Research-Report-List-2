<p><ul><li>Multiple Effect</li><li>Single Effect</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial vacuum evaporation systems are used for concentrating or distilling liquids in various industrial processes. There are two main types of these systems: multiple effect and single effect. </p><p>In a multiple effect system, the evaporated vapor from one stage is used to heat the next stage, resulting in higher efficiency and lower energy consumption. </p><p>In a single effect system, the process is simpler as only one stage is used for evaporation. However, it requires more energy to operate compared to a multiple effect system.</p></p>
