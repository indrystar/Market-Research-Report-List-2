<p><strong>The Dual-Fuel Marine Engines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Four-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines</li><li>Two-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dual-Fuel Marine Engines market is categorized into two types: Four-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines and Two-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines. Four-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines have a simpler design with a separate fuel injection system for gas and diesel. They offer higher efficiency and lower emissions. On the other hand, Two-Stroke Dual-Fuel Engines use a single fuel injection system for both gas and diesel. They are known for their compact size and lower fuel consumption. Both types are popular choices in the marine industry for their flexibility and environmental benefits.</p></p>
