<p><strong>The Perforated Computer Paper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Perforated computer paper is available in two main market types: single layer and multi-layer. Single layer paper consists of a single sheet with evenly spaced perforations for easy tearing. This type is commonly used for printing documents and reports. On the other hand, multi-layer paper includes several sheets that are interconnected with carbon paper in between, allowing for copies to be made while writing or typing on the top sheet. This type is often used for receipts, invoices, and other forms that require duplicate copies.</p></p>
