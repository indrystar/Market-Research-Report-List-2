They are commonly used in various industries such as automotive, construction, and medical. On the other hand, synthetic rubber tubes are manufactured chemically and are designed to have specific properties such as resistance to extreme temperatures, chemicals, and UV rays. They are used in applications where natural rubber tubes may not be suitable or cost-effective.</p></p>
