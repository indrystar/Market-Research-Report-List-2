<p><p>The concrete scanners market consists of two main types: integrated and portable scanners. Integrated scanners are permanently installed in a specific location for continuous monitoring of concrete structures. They are typically used in large construction projects or critical infrastructure. Portable scanners, on the other hand, are handheld devices that can be easily transported to different locations for on-site analysis of concrete structures. They are commonly used by construction professionals for quick and efficient scanning of smaller projects or areas where mobility is essential.</p></p>
