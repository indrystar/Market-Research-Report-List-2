<p><p>Gray cast iron is classified into different market types based on its microstructure. Ferrite market contains mostly ferrite, a soft and ductile phase, making the iron machinable and easily cast. Ferrite-Pearlite market contains a mixture of ferrite and pearlite, a harder phase, providing a balance of strength and machinability. Pearlite market consists mostly of pearlite, making the iron harder and less ductile but with improved wear resistance. Each market type has its own characteristics and is used for different applications based on their properties.</p></p>
