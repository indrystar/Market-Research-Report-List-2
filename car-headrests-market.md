<p><p>The car headrest market can be divided into two main types: integral car headrests and adjustable car headrests. Integral car headrests are built into the seat and cannot be adjusted or removed. They offer a fixed level of support for the neck and head while driving. On the other hand, adjustable car headrests can be customized to suit the individual's comfort preferences. They can be raised, lowered, tilted, or removed completely to provide optimal neck and head support for the driver and passengers.</p></p>
