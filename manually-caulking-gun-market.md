<p><p>Closed barrel caulking guns are designed with a sealed barrel that holds the caulk securely in place, preventing any leakage or drying out. This type of gun is generally easier to load and use, making it popular for quick and precise applications. On the other hand, open barrel caulking guns have a removable cartridge or tube for easy reloading and cleaning. This type allows for a wider range of caulk types and sizes to be used, but may be more prone to mess and drying out if not properly maintained.</p></p>
