<p><p>Medical carbon fiber composites are classified into two main types in the market: Thermosetting type and thermoplastic type. Thermosetting type composites are rigid and strong, making them ideal for applications requiring high stiffness and temperature resistance. On the other hand, thermoplastic type composites offer flexibility and are easily moldable, making them suitable for applications requiring complex shapes. Both types of composites are widely used in the medical industry for applications such as prosthetics, implants, and medical devices.</p></p>
