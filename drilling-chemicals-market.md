<p><strong>The Drilling Chemicals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Drilling Fluid</li><li>Completion Fluid</li><li>Grout</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Drilling chemicals market consists of three main types: drilling fluid, completion fluid, and grout. Drilling fluids are used to aid in the drilling process by lubricating the drill bit, carrying cuttings to the surface, and helping to maintain pressure in the wellbore. Completion fluids are used after drilling is complete to enhance production and protect the wellbore. Grout is used for sealing wellbores and preventing leaks. Each type of chemical plays a vital role in the drilling process, ensuring the success and longevity of the well.</p></p>
