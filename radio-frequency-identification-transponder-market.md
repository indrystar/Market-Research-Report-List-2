<p><p>The Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) Transponder market consists of three main types: Passive, Active, and Semi-Active. Passive RFID transponders do not have their own power source and rely on the energy from the RFID reader to transmit data. Active RFID transponders have a power source, enabling them to actively transmit data over longer distances. Semi-Active RFID transponders also have a power source, but only use it to power certain functions, such as sensors or alarms. Each type has its own distinct advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
