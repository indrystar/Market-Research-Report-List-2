<p><p>The paint auxiliary market comprises various types of products designed to improve the performance of paints and coatings. Rheology modifiers are used to control the flow and leveling of paints, while defoamers help to reduce the formation of foam during production and application. Dispersants aid in the dispersion of pigments and fillers, while wetting agents help to improve the wetting and adhesion of paints on different surfaces. Other types of auxiliaries include thickeners, biocides, and colorants, which also play a crucial role in enhancing the quality and functionality of paints.</p></p>
