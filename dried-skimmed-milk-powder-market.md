<p><p>Dried Skimmed Milk Powder (SMP) is categorized based on the heat treatment it undergoes during processing. Low Heat SMP is minimally processed to retain more of the milk's natural flavor and nutritional content. Medium Heat SMP goes through a slightly higher heat treatment, resulting in a slightly darker color and stronger flavor. High Heat SMP undergoes the highest level of heat treatment, which gives it a darker color, more intense flavor, and longer shelf life. Each type caters to different preferences and usage in various food applications.</p></p>
