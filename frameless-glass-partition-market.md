<p><p>Frameless Glass Partition Market can be divided into two main types: Single Glass Partition and Double Glass Partition. Single Glass Partition refers to a system that uses only one layer of glass to create a partition, providing a sleek and minimalistic look. On the other hand, Double Glass Partition involves using two layers of glass with a gap in between, offering increased sound insulation and thermal efficiency. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the market, providing versatile solutions for modern office spaces and residential areas.</p></p>
