<p><p>Digital ink market can be categorized into four types based on the type of ink used in printing: solvent-based, water-based, UV-cured, and others. Solvent-based inks use organic solvents for pigments and dyes, offering high durability but emit volatile organic compounds. Water-based inks are eco-friendly and non-toxic, making them ideal for food packaging. UV-cured inks are cured instantly using ultraviolet light, providing fast drying and high-quality prints. Others category includes specialty inks like latex and sublimation inks.</p></p>
