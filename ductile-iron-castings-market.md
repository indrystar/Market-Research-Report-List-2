<p><p>Ductile Iron Castings Market Types refer to the two main methods of casting ductile iron: Vertical Molding and Horizontal Molding. Vertical Molding involves pouring molten metal into a vertically positioned mold, while Horizontal Molding involves filling a horizontally positioned mold with molten metal. Both methods have their own advantages and disadvantages in terms of efficiency, cost-effectiveness, and quality of the final product. Companies in the ductile iron castings industry often choose between these two market types based on their specific production requirements and capabilities.</p></p>
