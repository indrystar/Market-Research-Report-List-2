<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>One-component adhesive accelerators are pre-mixed formulations that contain both the adhesive and the accelerator, simplifying the application process. They are ideal for quick and easy bonding in various industrial applications. Two-component adhesive accelerators require the separate mixing of adhesive and accelerator components before application. This allows for customization of the bonding properties and can be useful for more specific or complex bonding requirements. Both types of adhesive accelerators offer fast curing times and strong adhesive bonds, making them popular choices in the market.</p></p>
