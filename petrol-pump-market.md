<p><p>There are three main types of petrol pumps in the market: diaphragm, electric, and other. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to create suction and discharge fuel. Electric pumps use an electric motor to draw fuel from the tank and dispense it into the vehicle. The "other" category may include specialized pumps designed for specific applications or alternative fuel types. Each type of pump has its own unique features and benefits, catering to different needs and preferences in the fueling market.</p></p>
