<p><strong>The Aluminum Brazing Sheets Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Torch Brazing</li><li>Induction Brazing</li><li>Furnace Brazing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum brazing sheets find application in torch brazing, induction brazing, and furnace brazing processes. Torch brazing involves using a handheld torch to heat the brazing material and create a bond between the sheets. Induction brazing uses electromagnetic induction to heat the material, enabling precise control of temperature and minimizing distortion. Furnace brazing involves placing the sheets in a controlled atmosphere furnace to create a strong bond. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, precision, and quality of the brazed joints.</p></p>
