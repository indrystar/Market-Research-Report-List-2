<p><p>Hydraulic lift buffers are crucial components in hydraulic lifts, providing cushioning and stability during use. There are three main types of buffers commonly used in the market: polyurethane buffers, spring buffers, and oil buffers. Polyurethane buffers are known for their durability and resistance to wear and tear. Spring buffers offer a balance of flexibility and load-bearing capacity. Oil buffers use hydraulic fluid to absorb and dissipate energy, providing smooth and controlled movements. Each type has unique applications and advantages in various hydraulic lift systems.</p></p>
