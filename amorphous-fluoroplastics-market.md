<p><p>Amorphous fluoroplastics are available in two main market types: powdered resin and solution. Powdered resin refers to the form of fluoroplastics in which the material is in a solid, powdered state that can be molded or extruded to create various products. On the other hand, solution market involves the dispersion of fluoroplastics in a liquid solution, which allows for easy application on surfaces or for coating purposes. Both market types offer versatility and unique properties of amorphous fluoroplastics for different applications.</p></p>
