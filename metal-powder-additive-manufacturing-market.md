<p><strong>The Metal Powder Additive Manufacturing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li><li>Electronic Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal powder additive manufacturing market types include Selective Laser Melting (SLM) and Electronic Beam Melting (EBM). SLM uses a high-power laser to selectively melt and fuse metal powder particles together to create the desired part. EBM employs an electron beam to melt and solidify metal powder layer by layer. Other market types may include Direct Energy Deposition and Binder Jetting, which also utilize metal powder to build parts layer by layer, but with different additive manufacturing processes.</p></p>
