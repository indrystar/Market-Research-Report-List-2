<p><p>Polyurethane systems market can be divided into two main types: Thermoplastic and Thermosetting. Thermoplastic polyurethane systems are able to be melted and reformed multiple times, making them ideal for applications requiring flexibility and resilience. On the other hand, thermosetting polyurethane systems undergo a chemical reaction during curing, resulting in a rigid and durable final product. These systems are often used in applications requiring high strength and durability, such as in construction materials and automotive components.</p></p>
