Combustion Technology involves burning wood biomass to produce heat and electricity. Co-Firing involves blending wood biomass with traditional fossil fuels to reduce emissions and reliance on non-renewable resources. Cogeneration Technologies involve using wood biomass to produce both heat and electricity simultaneously, increasing efficiency and reducing waste. These technologies play a key role in promoting renewable energy sources and reducing greenhouse gas emissions.</p></p>
