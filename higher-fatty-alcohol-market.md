<p><p>Higher Fatty Alcohol market is categorized into Short Chain, Long Chain, and Higher Chain based on the number of carbon atoms in their molecular structure. Short Chain fatty alcohols have 1-6 carbon atoms, Long Chain have 7-12 carbon atoms, and Higher Chain have 13 or more carbon atoms. Short Chain fatty alcohols are commonly used in personal care products, Long Chain fatty alcohols are used in detergents and industrial applications, while Higher Chain fatty alcohols find applications in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics.</p></p>
