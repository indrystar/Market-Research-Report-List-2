<p><p>UV measurement strips come in various types to measure different ranges of ultraviolet (UV) radiation. UVA strips measure the long-wave UVA radiation, which is commonly associated with skin aging. UVB strips measure the medium-wave UVB radiation, which is responsible for sunburn. UVC strips measure the short-wave UVC radiation, which is typically filtered out by the Earth's ozone layer. There are also other types of UV measurement strips available to measure specific UV ranges or for specialized applications. Each type of strip provides valuable information on the intensity of UV exposure.</p></p>
