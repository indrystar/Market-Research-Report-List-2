<p><ul><li>Double Strand Break Repair</li><li>Engineered Nucleases</li><li>Clustered Regularly Interspaced Short Palindromic Repeats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gene Editing Technologies market can be categorized into three main types: Double Strand Break Repair, Engineered Nucleases, and Clustered Regularly Interspaced Short Palindromic Repeats (CRISPR). Double Strand Break Repair involves repairing the break in DNA strands using specific enzymes. Engineered Nucleases are customized enzymes designed to target specific sequences in the genome for editing. CRISPR technology uses RNA-guided nucleases to make precise edits in the DNA sequence. These technologies are revolutionizing the field of genetic engineering and have a wide range of applications in healthcare, agriculture, and biotechnology.</p></p>
