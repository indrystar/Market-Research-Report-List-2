<p><ul><li>Array Process Testing Equipment</li><li>Cell / Module Process Testing Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Array Process Testing Equipment is used for inspecting the array process of display panels, ensuring the accuracy and quality of the array assembly. This equipment typically includes tools for detecting defects in the array such as misalignments, defects in pixel formation, and color variations. On the other hand, Cell/Module Process Testing Equipment is used for inspecting the individual cells or modules of the display panels, focusing on the quality and functionality of each cell/module before the final assembly of the display panel.</p></p>
