<p><p>Optogenetics market includes three main types: Viral Vectors, Cre-Dependant Expression System, and Transgenic Animals. Viral vectors are commonly used to deliver light-sensitive proteins to specific cells in the brain. Cre-Dependant Expression Systems allow for precise control over gene expression in targeted neurons. Transgenic Animals are genetically modified organisms that express light-sensitive proteins, enabling researchers to study neural circuits and behavior. These methods collectively provide powerful tools for studying and manipulating brain activity with light.</p></p>
