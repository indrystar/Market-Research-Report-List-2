<p><p>Shave foam market can be divided into two main types: aftershave and pre-shave products. Aftershave products are used post-shave to soothe the skin, reduce irritation, and provide a refreshing scent. These products often contain ingredients like alcohol, witch hazel, and essential oils. On the other hand, pre-shave products are used before shaving to soften the beard, hydrate the skin, and create a smooth surface for the razor. These products typically contain moisturizing agents, such as glycerin and aloe vera, to prepare the skin for a close and comfortable shave.</p></p>
