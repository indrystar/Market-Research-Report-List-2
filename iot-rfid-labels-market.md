<p><strong>The IoT RFID Labels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Labels</li><li>Active Labels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IoT RFID Labels market is segmented into two main types: Passive Labels and Active Labels. Passive labels do not have a power source and rely on the RFID reader for power, making them more cost-effective for tracking and monitoring. Active labels have their own power source, allowing them to transmit data over longer distances and provide more real-time monitoring capabilities. Both types cater to different market needs, with passive labels being suited for simple tracking applications and active labels offering more advanced functionalities.</p></p>
