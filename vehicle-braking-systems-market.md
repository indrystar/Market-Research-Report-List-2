<p><p>Vehicle braking systems market is divided into two main types: drum brake and disc brake. Drum brakes are enclosed within a drum and work by pressing outward against the drum when the brake pedal is pressed. Disc brakes, on the other hand, use pads to clamp onto a rotor to slow down the vehicle. Both types have their own advantages and disadvantages, and their popularity depends on factors such as cost, performance, and ease of maintenance.</p></p>
