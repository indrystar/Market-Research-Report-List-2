<p><p>Industrial catalytic converters are crucial components used in industries to reduce harmful emissions. The two-way converter is designed to oxidize carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons into carbon dioxide and water. On the other hand, the three-way converter additionally reduces nitrogen oxides into nitrogen and oxygen. Two-way converters are commonly used in diesel engines, while three-way converters are more prevalent in gasoline engines. Both converters play a vital role in promoting environmental sustainability by minimizing pollutants released into the atmosphere.</p></p>
