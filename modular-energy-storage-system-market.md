<p><p>A Modular Energy Storage System (MESS) is an energy storage solution that consists of multiple smaller, interconnected energy storage units. These units can be added or removed as needed, allowing for flexibility and scalability in energy storage capacity. MESS are typically used to store electricity generated from renewable sources such as solar or wind power, and can also be used for grid stabilization and peak shaving.</p><p>The Modular Energy Storage System Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 4.7% during the forecast period.
