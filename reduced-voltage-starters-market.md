<p><p>Reduced Voltage Starters are commonly available in two types: Single Phase and Three Phase. Single Phase starters are designed for motors that operate on a single-phase power supply, typically used in small household appliances and some industrial equipment. On the other hand, Three Phase starters are suitable for motors that run on a three-phase power supply, commonly found in industrial and commercial applications. Both types of reduced voltage starters help to reduce electrical stress and mechanical wear on motors during startup, leading to improved efficiency and longer motor lifespan.</p></p>
