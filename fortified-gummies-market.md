<p><p>Fortified gummies are a popular type of supplement that come in two main market types: Pectin-based and Gelatin-based. Pectin-based gummies are made from fruit-derived pectin instead of animal-derived gelatin, making them suitable for vegetarians and vegans. Gelatin-based gummies are more common and are derived from animal collagen. While both types offer a convenient and tasty way to consume vitamins and minerals, the choice between pectin-based and gelatin-based gummies ultimately depends on dietary preferences and restrictions.</p></p>
